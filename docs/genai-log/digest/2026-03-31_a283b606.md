## Summary

This session covered the most architecturally significant work of the period: a clean-slate redesign of pane's architecture driven by two weeks of accumulated insight. Lane directed implementation of the C1 calloop looper Phase 2 and Phase 3, then pivoted to a multi-day multi-agent deliberation on whether the prototype architecture should be scrapped in favor of a fresh design using the `par` crate's CLL-based session types and the EAct actor model. Lane articulated the decision criterion: the current codebase was a proof of concept, not a commitment, and if the agents could produce a more principled design taking into account pane-headless, the distributed namespace insight, and the BeOS/Plan 9 unifications discovered during development, a rewrite was warranted. By the end of the session the architecture.md, ai-kit.md, and major supporting docs were all updated to the new standard.
