Key Finding

The Transport trait in pane-session is the critical abstraction. Currently two impls exist:

  • UnixTransport — production IPC (unix domain sockets)
  • MemoryTransport — testing (mpsc channels)

Adding a TcpTransport (or TLS-wrapped variant) would make the entire green stack network-transparent with zero protocol changes. The session types, handshake, and active-phase enums are already serialized via postcard over length-prefixed frames.
